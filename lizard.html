Lizard

Specialties

  • Camouflage: Chameleons can change colors to match their environment. However, many chameleons are limited to a few colors, making blending into backgrounds difficult.
  • Eyes: Chameleons can rotate their eyes 180 degrees, giving them 360 degree vision. They can also transition between monocular and binocular vision.
  • Feet: Chameleons have zygodactylous feet that split at nearly 180 degrees, giving them a good grip on branches.
  • Tongue: Chameleons have tongues that are almost twice their body length and are used to catch prey

5 interesting facts about lizard

  1. Lizards are cold blooded and rely on external heat sources like the sun to regulate their body temperature.
  2. Some lizards like chameleons and anoles can change their colors from bright green to brown to communicate with each other or for camouflage.
  3. Some lizards can detach their tails to escape predators and regenerate them in a month.
  4. Most lizards have eyelids to protect their eyes, but some lizards like geckos have a transparent membrane to shield their eyes and use their tongues to clean them.
  5. Some lizards can run on water, like the basilisk lizard, which can run up to 20 meters on water as hatchlings.
