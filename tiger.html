Tiger

Specialties

  • Striped camouflage: The only wild cat species with fully striped fur, which helps them blend in with their environment and sneak up on prey
  • Strength and size: Tigers are the largest big cats, with males weighing over 500 pounds, and have immense power to take down large prey
  • Stealth and agility: Tigers are remarkably agile and can move swiftly when needed
  • Powerful jaws and teeth: Tigers have formidable jaws and long, sharp teeth designed for delivering a swift and lethal bite to their prey

5 interesting facts about tigers

  1. Tigers are the largest of all the wild cats. Male Royal Bengal Tigers can weigh up to 300 kilograms.
  2. Tigers are good swimmers and enjoy spending time in water.
  3. Tigers have soft toe pads, which help them walk silently through their habitat.
  4. Tigers have excellent night vision, which is 5-6 times better than humans.
  5. Tigers are solitary hunters and their hunting strategy focuses on stalking their prey.
